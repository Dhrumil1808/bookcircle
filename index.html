Why Circle?

Problem: In a growing society that is increasingly reliant on technology, many people have stopped reading.

Solution: Our solution is a platform that creates a seamless reading experience for everyone. This product will offer useful tools for reading productivity, create a local community of readers and influencers, and expose users to books best suited for them.

Notable Features

Check out what you can do with our amazing app!

Circle

Read user profiles which includes Books they read, reading speed, and their reflections. Watch celebrities using Books.

Discover

Displays topics you follow: reflections, books, genres

Read

Camera dictionary. Stop notifications (DND feature switched on.) A feedback of how you did compared to yesterday/last week after you enter the last page number and finish reading.

Growth

Personal stats on reading, vocab, reading speed, number of books read, projected books you want to read, desired speed you want to achieve, goals
